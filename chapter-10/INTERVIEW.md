# Chapter 10 Interview Questions

## Q1: Permission 777 risk?
**A:** Anyone can read, write, execute - security risk

## Q2: Immutable file?
**A:** chattr +i - cannot be modified even by root

## Q3: Fail2ban purpose?
**A:** Blocks IPs after failed login attempts

## Q4: sudo vs root login?
**A:** sudo provides accountability, root is permanent
